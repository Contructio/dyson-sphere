#### 📜 Эффект перка
You are artificial being. You don't eat, sleep or breathe. You can't reproduce or regenerate. Instead of healing, you must find spare parts. Any wound is fixed by engineering skill instead of medicine. You consume 8 Power Peels worth of energy per 24 hour cycle. If you decide to hibernate for 8 hours a day, your consumption decreases to 5 PP's. You can hibernate for 10 years on 1 PP.
